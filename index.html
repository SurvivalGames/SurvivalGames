Welcome to The Survival Games

A new innovative survival games plugin made by the community and for the community, complete with a party manager and inbuilt multiworld support

Our goal is to create the best survival games plugin out there, properly maintained, unlike many other plugins out there

Party System

Parties can be used to chat with your friends, and join the same games, without having to rush click the same sign.

Multiworld

Each SG arena has its own world and configuration file. This allows for better origination, and cross sever map sharing.

Signs

Each arena will have a join sign, when you click the sign, you (and your party members) will have there inventory cleared and save, and be teleported to the lobby.

Points

Points are the currency of the Survival Games. Points are gained when a player gets a kill, or another player sponsors and bounties from an in game player. The points are displayed in front of the players name tag when chatting. Points can be adjusted with commands by admins when needed, or directly through SQL querys

Bountying

Bountying will happen throughout the game by players and spectators, when a player or spectator types /sg bounty [points] the points will be taken from their profile and put on the other players name. When a player with a bounty on them is killed, the killer will receive the points added onto the points gained from killing a player. If they player does not die in the whole game then they will get the points added onto their points total.

Sponsoring

When you are spectating a game you can sponsor a player with /sg sponsor [points]. The player will get a random item with a value according to the number of points the player used in the sponsor command.

Special Items

Our Survival Games plugin features many ingame items top help you defeat your enemies.

  • Snowballs and Sugar, slow and speed up.
  • Insta-TNT, and much much more
